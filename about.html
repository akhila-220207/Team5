Our Story & Mission

Bal Raksha Bharat is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating a world where every child attains the right to survival, protection, development, and participation.

Major Activities

Quality Education

We establish community learning centers and provide school kits to ensure that financial barriers don't stop a child's education.

Health & Nutrition

Conducting regular health check-ups and providing mid-day meals to combat malnutrition in underprivileged urban and rural areas.

Child Protection

Running awareness campaigns against child labor and early marriage, creating safe zones for children to play and grow.

WASH Initiatives

Providing clean drinking water and sanitation facilities in remote villages to prevent water-borne diseases among children.
